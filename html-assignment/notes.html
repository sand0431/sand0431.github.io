Header tag
Header tag use for heading
Nav tag
The navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website
Article tag
Article tag is used for containing independent piece of content. It could be shared as stand alone piece of content and will still make sense.
Aside tag
Secondary content on the page, this information is not part of the main topic of the page and is not required.
Div tag
Div is used to wrap around multiple child elements. Div divides the content into logical groups which will show the content on a separate line.
